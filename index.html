Video Editing Portfolio

League of Legends 2017 Summer Playoffs Hype Trailer

This video was created before the League of Legends 2017 Summer Playoffs. I wanted to utilize the song Legend by The Score, because I felt that it fit perfectly with the narrative of all the teams that were in the playoffs, and how badly all of them wanted to win it. My goal with this video was to get people excited for the playoffs and remember all the great moments that led up to this moment.

Team Solomid - Against All Odds - Spring 2016 Finals Hype Trailer

This was one of my first videos that I had ever created. With Team Solomid being one of my favorite teams, I wanted to create a video that immortalized the current roster. It was a super team that had been created, but from the beginning had many challenges. I wanted to show the journey of the team, who started off favorites in the pre-season and then underdogs going into the playoffs who had now made it to the finals. This video was to inspire the fans before their finals.
